# BagBoard

A call CRM for people who dial for a living. One HTML file. No login, no servers,
no monthly bill - your leads live in your own browser and you call them from your
own phone. Works for any kind of phone outreach: sales, follow-ups, scheduling,
collections — anything you work down a list to do.

## What it does
- Lines up who to call next and runs you through them one at a time - number, outcome, next.
- Moves each lead through New, Contacted, Callback, Appt, Quoted, Closed, and schedules the follow-up for you.
- On a phone, tap to call or text. On a computer, it copies the number so you can dial by hand and copies a ready-to-send follow-up text.
- Search, sort, filter, multi-select, and de-dupe your list.
- An agenda of what's overdue and what's coming up.
- Stats that mean something: contact rate, appointment rate, a 7-day call chart, pipeline by stage, and personal bests.
- Keeps you in the loop: a daily-goal ring, streaks, in-session momentum, milestone moments, and subtle synthesized sound cues (toggleable).
- Import a CSV, export a CSV, download a backup. Nothing leaves your machine.

## Run it
Open `index.html` in any browser and start working.

To run it as a local app on Windows (data saves more reliably, native copy), double-click
`Start-BagBoard-Windows.bat`. It serves the app at http://localhost:8753 using PowerShell
that's already on every Windows PC - no install, no admin. Close the window to stop.
`SETUP-GUIDE.md` has the click-by-click version.

## Your data
Stored in your browser's localStorage on the device you use, and nowhere else. Clear your
browser and it's gone, so download a backup from Settings now and then.
